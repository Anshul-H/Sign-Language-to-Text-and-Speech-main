================================================================================
HOW IT WORKS (SIMPLE)
================================================================================

1. Camera captures your hand
2. MediaPipe finds 21 points on hand
3. Points converted to 42 numbers
4. ML model predicts gesture (A-Z, 0-9, SPACE, FULLSTOP)
5. System waits for stable prediction (1.5 seconds)
6. Character appears on screen
7. Characters form words and sentences
8. Text-to-speech reads it aloud
9. Save your session to file

================================================================================
TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
================================================================================

1. Use bright, even lighting
2. Plain background (solid color)
3. Keep hand centered in frame
4. Hold gesture steady for 1.5 seconds
5. Watch confidence meter (aim for >80%)
6. Distance: 1-2 feet from camera
7. Ensure all fingers visible

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Low FPS
Fix: Close other apps, toggle landmarks off

Problem: Low confidence
Fix: Better lighting, correct gesture shape

Problem: No detection
Fix: Check camera, ensure hand in frame

Problem: Duplicate characters
Fix: Wait 1.5 seconds between same gestures

Problem: No speech
Fix: Check audio settings, try different voice
